<p><strong>The Fume Chamber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Chamber</li><li>Ducted Fume Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fume chambers are essential for laboratory safety, designed to protect users from hazardous fumes and vapors. The market is primarily divided into two types: ductless fume chambers and ducted fume chambers. Ductless fume chambers use integrated filtration systems to purify air before recirculating it back into the room, making them ideal for locations without ventilation access. Conversely, ducted fume chambers exhaust harmful gases directly outside, providing superior ventilation and safety, suitable for environments requiring higher levels of fume containment.</p></p>
