<p><p>Clamshell packaging, widely used for retail and consumer goods, is available in various materials, each with distinct properties. **PE (Polyethylene)** offers flexibility and durability, ideal for lightweight products. **PP (Polypropylene)** provides high clarity and resistance to heat, suitable for food and medical items. **PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate)** is transparent, strong, and recyclable, often used for electronics and cosmetics. **PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)** is rigid and cost-effective but less eco-friendly. **Others** include biodegradable or composite materials catering to sustainability trends. Each type balances functionality, cost, and environmental impact.</p></p>
