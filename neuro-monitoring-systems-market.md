<p><p>Neuro Monitoring Systems encompass various market types including Sub-Sensory Evoked Potentials, which measure neural responses to stimuli below conscious perception; Brainstem Auditory Evoked Potentials that assess brain activity in response to auditory stimuli; Visual Evoked Potentials, which evaluate the brain's response to visual signals; and Somatosensory-Evoked Potentials, focusing on the processing of sensory information from the body. These systems are essential for monitoring neurological function during surgeries and assessing conditions that affect sensory pathways.</p></p>
