<p><p>The automatic hand dryers market consists of two primary types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use powerful jets of air to rapidly dry hands in a short time, offering efficiency and reduced energy consumption. In contrast, hot air dryers provide a warm stream of air that gently evaporates moisture, typically taking longer to dry hands but often being quieter and less aggressive. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences in terms of speed, comfort, and hygiene.</p></p>
