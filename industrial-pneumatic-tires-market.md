Traditional pneumatic tires, filled with air, provide better cushioning and shock absorption, leading to improved ride comfort and traction on various surfaces. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, influencing performance, safety, and operational efficiency in equipment like forklifts and construction vehicles.</p></p>
