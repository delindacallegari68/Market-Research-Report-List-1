<p><p>The automotive manual transmission fluid market comprises various grades, including GL-1 to GL-5, each indicating distinct performance characteristics. GL-1 is a basic, non-synthetic oil for older transmissions, while GL-2 and GL-3 offer improved oxidation stability and wear protection. GL-4 is formulated for moderate-pressure gear applications, balancing protection and compatibility. GL-5 provides high levels of extreme-pressure protection, suitable for modern gears under heavy loads. The differentiation helps consumers select fluids based on vehicle requirements and driving conditions.</p></p>
