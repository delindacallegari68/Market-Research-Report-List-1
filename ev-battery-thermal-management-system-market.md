<p><strong>The EV Battery Thermal Management System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Management System</li><li>Passive Management System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EV Battery Thermal Management System market comprises two main types: active and passive management systems. Active management systems utilize components like coolants, heaters, and pumps to regulate battery temperature, ensuring optimal performance and safety under varying conditions. Conversely, passive management systems rely on materials and structural design to naturally dissipate heat or insulate the battery, requiring no external energy sources. Both systems aim to enhance battery life and efficiency, catering to diverse electric vehicle applications and environmental challenges.</p></p>
