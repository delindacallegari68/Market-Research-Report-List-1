Autogenous mills use the ore itself as grinding media, providing efficient size reduction with minimal external input. In contrast, semiautogenous mills incorporate a mixture of ore and steel balls, enhancing the grinding process through both impact and abrasion. This market serves various industries, including mining and mineral processing, where the demand for optimized milling solutions drives innovation and technology advancements in grinding equipment.</p></p>
