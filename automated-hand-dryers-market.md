<p><p>The automated hand dryers market features two main types: jet air dryers and hot air dryers. Jet air dryers use powerful streams of air to rapidly evaporate moisture, offering faster drying times and often being more hygienic due to their touchless operation. In contrast, hot air dryers utilize warm air to dry hands gradually, typically taking longer but being more energy-efficient. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and settings, such as commercial restrooms and public facilities, focusing on convenience and hygiene.</p></p>
