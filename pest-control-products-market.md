<p><p>The pest control products market comprises three main types: Chemical Control, Mechanical Control, and Biological Control. Chemical Control involves the use of synthetic pesticides to eliminate pests effectively. Mechanical Control employs physical methods, such as traps and barriers, to manage pest populations without chemicals. Biological Control utilizes natural predators, parasites, or pathogens to suppress pest populations, promoting ecological balance. Each method offers distinct advantages and is chosen based on the pest type, environmental impact, and specific pest control needs.</p></p>
