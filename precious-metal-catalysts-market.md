<p><p>Precious metal catalysts are materials that use precious metals such as platinum, palladium, rhodium, gold, and silver to accelerate chemical reactions without being consumed in the process. These catalysts are widely used in industries such as automotive, chemical manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, and environmental protection due to their high efficiency, stability, and ability to operate under extreme conditions. In the automotive sector, they are crucial for catalytic converters, which reduce harmful emissions. In chemical manufacturing, they facilitate processes like hydrogenation and oxidation.</p><p>The Precious Metal Catalysts Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.3% during the forecast period.
