<p><p>The ceramic dryer market is primarily divided into two types: horizontal and vertical dryers. Horizontal dryers utilize a flat orientation, promoting even airflow and uniform drying of ceramic materials, making them suitable for large-scale production. In contrast, vertical dryers operate with a vertical orientation, allowing for efficient space utilization and quicker moisture removal from ceramics. Both types cater to different manufacturing needs, with horizontal dryers focusing on bulk processing and vertical dryers emphasizing compact designs and faster drying cycles.</p></p>
