<p><p>The high voltage cable market is primarily divided into two types: AC power cables and DC power cables. AC power cables are utilized for transmitting alternating current in power distribution networks, typically suited for medium to long distances. They are essential for connecting substations and facilitating grid stability. In contrast, DC power cables are designed for direct current transmission, making them ideal for renewable energy sources like solar and wind, as well as long-distance undersea cables. Both types play crucial roles in modern electrical infrastructure.</p></p>
