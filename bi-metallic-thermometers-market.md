<p><p>Bi-metallic thermometers are temperature measuring instruments that operate based on the principle of thermal expansion. They utilize two bonded metal strips with different coefficients of expansion, which bend in response to temperature changes, allowing for accurate temperature readings. This type of thermometer is widely used across various industries, including food and beverage, HVAC, automotive, and manufacturing, due to its durability and reliability.</p><p>The Bi-Metallic Thermometers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.9% during the forecast period.
