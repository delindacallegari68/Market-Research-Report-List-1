<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Automotive Catalytic Converter Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three-way Catalysts (TWC)</li><li>Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC)</li><li>Lean NOx Trap (LNT)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive catalytic converter market includes various types designed to reduce harmful emissions. **Three-way Catalysts (TWC)** target CO, HC, and NOx in gasoline engines. **Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC)** reduce NOx in lean-burn engines. **Lean NOx Trap (LNT)** temporarily stores NOx for later reduction. **Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)** uses urea to convert NOx into nitrogen and water in diesel engines. **Diesel Oxidation Catalyst (DOC)** oxidizes CO and HC into CO2 and water, primarily for diesel vehicles. Each type addresses specific pollutants, ensuring compliance with emission standards.</p></p>
