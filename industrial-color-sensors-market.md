<p><p>The industrial color sensors market is primarily classified into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel sensors capture one specific wavelength, making them ideal for basic color detection tasks and simple applications. In contrast, multi-channel sensors measure multiple wavelengths simultaneously, enabling them to analyze a broader color spectrum. This versatility allows for more precise applications, such as quality control and sorting in manufacturing processes. The choice between them depends on the complexity and accuracy required for specific industrial tasks.</p></p>
