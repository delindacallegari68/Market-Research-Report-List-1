<p><p>UVGI (Ultraviolet Germicidal Irradiation) technology is categorized into three main market types: air sanitation, surface sanitation, and water sanitation. Air sanitation utilizes UVGI to eliminate airborne pathogens in HVAC systems and indoor spaces, enhancing indoor air quality. Surface sanitation employs UVGI to disinfect high-touch surfaces in healthcare, food processing, and public areas, reducing the risk of contamination. Water sanitation applies UVGI to treat drinking and wastewater, effectively destroying microorganisms without chemicals, promoting safe water supply and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
