<p><p>The mineral processing market encompasses various segment types essential for resource extraction and preparation. Crushing involves breaking down large ore into smaller pieces for easier handling. Screening separates materials based on size, ensuring the right particle dimensions for further processing. Grinding reduces particle size through mechanical processes, enhancing the liberation of valuable minerals. Classification focuses on separating particles based on characteristics like size and density, optimizing the efficiency of subsequent processing stages. Together, these processes support effective mineral extraction and utilization.</p></p>
