<p><strong>The Organic Light-emitting Diodes (OLEDs）Microdisplay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Microdisplay</li><li>Transmissive Microdisplay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED microdisplay market comprises two primary types: reflective and transmissive microdisplays. Reflective microdisplays use ambient light to illuminate the OLED layers, making them suitable for applications like augmented reality where power efficiency is crucial. In contrast, transmissive microdisplays allow light to pass through the OLED layers, offering superior brightness and color accuracy, ideal for virtual reality and high-resolution viewing. Both types cater to different use cases and performance requirements in various sectors, including gaming and wearable technology.</p></p>
