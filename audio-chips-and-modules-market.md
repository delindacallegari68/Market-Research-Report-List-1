<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Audio Chips and Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Audio Amplifiers</li><li>Audio CODECs</li><li>Audio DSPs</li><li>Audio SoCs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The audio chips and modules market consists of several key types: </p><p>1. **Audio Amplifiers** increase sound power for various applications, enhancing audio playback. </p><p>2. **Audio CODECs** convert analog signals to digital and vice versa, crucial for sound processing. </p><p>3. **Audio DSPs** (Digital Signal Processors) manage audio data manipulation for effects and improvements. </p><p>4. **Audio SoCs** (System-on-Chips) integrate multiple audio functions into a single chip, optimizing space and performance in devices like smartphones and IoT gadgets. Each type plays a vital role in advancing audio technology.</p></p>
