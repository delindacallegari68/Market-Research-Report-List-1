<p><strong>The Ultra Heat Treatment (UHT) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Heating UHT System</li><li>Indirect Heating UHT System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Heat Treatment (UHT) system market includes two primary types: Direct Heating UHT Systems and Indirect Heating UHT Systems. Direct Heating UHT Systems rapidly heat liquids by directly injecting steam, ensuring efficient thermal processing and quick sterilization. In contrast, Indirect Heating UHT Systems utilize heat exchangers to transfer heat, providing greater control over temperature and minimizing the risk of altering the product's flavor. Both systems are vital for extending shelf life and ensuring food safety in the dairy and beverage industries.</p></p>
