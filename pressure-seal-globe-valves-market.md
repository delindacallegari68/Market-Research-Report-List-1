<p><p>The pressure seal globe valves market consists of various connection types, notably socket weld and butt weld connections. Socket weld connections involve attaching pipes by inserting them into a valve socket and welding around the joint, offering ease of installation and maintenance. In contrast, butt weld connections require the pipe ends to be aligned and welded directly to the valve, providing a robust and durable connection ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types cater to industries needing reliable flow control and leakage resistance.</p></p>
