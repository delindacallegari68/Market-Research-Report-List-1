<p><p>The Controlled and Slow Release Fertilizers market encompasses various types that enhance nutrient uptake efficiency. Organic low-solubility fertilizers are derived from natural sources, slowly releasing nutrients as they decompose. Inorganic low-solubility fertilizers are synthesized chemicals that maintain nutrient availability over time, minimizing losses. Coated and encapsulated fertilizers, typically featuring a protective layer, control the release rate further, ensuring consistent nutrient delivery based on environmental conditions. Together, these types cater to sustainable agriculture by optimizing nutrient management.</p></p>
