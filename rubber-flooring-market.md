<p><p>The rubber flooring market is primarily categorized into two main types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber is derived from the sap of rubber trees, known for its durability, elasticity, and eco-friendliness, making it suitable for various applications. In contrast, synthetic rubber, produced from petroleum-based materials, offers a wider range of properties, including enhanced resistance to chemicals and UV light, and is often more economical. Both types cater to diverse sectors, including commercial, residential, and industrial flooring solutions.</p></p>
