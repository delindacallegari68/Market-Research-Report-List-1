<p><p>The cyanoacrylate adhesive market includes three key types: alkoxy-ethyl-based, ethyl-ester-based, and methyl-ester-based adhesives. Alkoxy-ethyl-based adhesives offer flexibility and resistance to moisture, making them suitable for a variety of applications. Ethyl-ester-based adhesives provide strong bonding with quick curing times, ideal for industrial uses. Methyl-ester-based adhesives are known for their excellent adhesion to plastics and rubber but can be less flexible. Each type caters to specific requirements in sectors like automotive, packaging, and healthcare.</p></p>
