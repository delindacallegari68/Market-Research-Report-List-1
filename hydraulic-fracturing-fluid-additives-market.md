<p><p>The hydraulic fracturing fluid additives market includes several key types that enhance fracture efficiency and protect equipment. Corrosion inhibitors prevent pipe degradation during fluid transport. Biocides eliminate microbial growth that could affect fluid performance. Crosslinkers enhance the viscosity and stability of the fracturing fluid at high temperatures, improving proppant transport. Surfactants reduce surface tension, enhancing the fluid's ability to penetrate rock formations. Other additives may include fluid loss control agents and pH adjusters, each contributing to optimal fracturing performance.</p></p>
