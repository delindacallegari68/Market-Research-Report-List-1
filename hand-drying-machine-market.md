<p><strong>The Hand Drying Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hand-in</li><li>Hand-under</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand drying machine market is primarily categorized into two types: hand-in and hand-under models. Hand-in machines require users to insert their hands into a slot, allowing for focused air drying, often resulting in quicker drying times. Conversely, hand-under machines enable users to place their hands underneath, promoting a more natural drying motion but typically taking longer. Both types cater to various user preferences in public restrooms, emphasizing efficiency, hygiene, and ease of use in busy environments.</p></p>
