<p><p>Thin Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Displays (TFT-LCDs) are categorized into three primary types: Twisted Nematic (TN), Vertical Alignment (VA), and In-Plane Switching (IPS). TN panels are known for their fast response times and lower production costs, typically used in budget displays. VA panels offer better contrast and color reproduction, making them suitable for immersive viewing. IPS panels excel in color accuracy and wide viewing angles, ideal for professional applications and high-end devices, enhancing overall user experience across various markets.</p></p>
