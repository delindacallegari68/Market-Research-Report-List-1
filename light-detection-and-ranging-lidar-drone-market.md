<p><p>The LiDAR drone market comprises two primary types: rotary-wing and fixed-wing LiDAR drones. Rotary-wing LiDAR drones, commonly known as quadcopters or multi-rotors, offer high maneuverability and are ideal for surveying small or complex areas, providing detailed, high-resolution data. In contrast, fixed-wing LiDAR drones are designed for efficiency over large areas, capable of covering greater distances and flying longer durations. Both types serve various applications, including forestry, agriculture, and infrastructure mapping, catering to diverse surveying needs.</p></p>
