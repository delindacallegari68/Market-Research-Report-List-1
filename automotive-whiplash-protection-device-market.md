<p><p>An Automotive Whiplash Protection Device (AWPD) is a safety mechanism designed to reduce the risk of whiplash injuries during rear-end collisions. These devices are integrated into vehicle seats and work by minimizing the sudden backward and forward motion of the head and neck, which is a common cause of whiplash. Technologies such as active head restraints, energy-absorbing materials, and advanced seat designs are commonly used in AWPDs to enhance passenger safety.</p><p>The Automotive Whiplash Protection Device Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of vehicle safety and stringent government regulations mandating advanced safety features in automobiles.
