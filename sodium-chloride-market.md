<p><p>The sodium chloride market is categorized into three main types: solar salt, rock salt, and vacuum salt. Solar salt is produced through the evaporation of seawater, resulting in large, coarse crystals typically used for de-icing and food processing. Rock salt is mined from underground deposits and is often used for industrial applications and de-icing roads. Vacuum salt, created by evaporating brine in vacuum conditions, offers high purity and is commonly used in pharmaceuticals and food production. Each type serves distinct industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
