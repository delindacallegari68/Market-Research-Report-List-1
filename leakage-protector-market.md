<p><p>The leakage protector market can be categorized into three main types: Voltage Type, Current Type, and Pulse Type. </p><p>Voltage Type monitors voltage levels and cuts off the supply when leakage is detected, ensuring safety. Current Type focuses on measuring the current flowing through the circuit, activating when an imbalance occurs, thus preventing hazards. Pulse Type uses short electrical pulses to detect leakage, providing rapid response and enhanced protection against electrical faults. Each type serves to enhance safety in electrical systems effectively.</p></p>
