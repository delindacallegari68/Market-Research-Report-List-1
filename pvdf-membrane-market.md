<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic</li><li>Hydrophilic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PVDF membrane market is primarily categorized into hydrophobic and hydrophilic types, each serving distinct applications. Hydrophobic PVDF membranes repel water, making them ideal for filtration of gases and non-aqueous solutions, while also providing superior chemical resistance. Conversely, hydrophilic PVDF membranes attract water, facilitating the filtration of aqueous-based solutions, and are widely used in water treatment and biopharmaceutical applications. Both types leverage PVDF's excellent mechanical and thermal stability, catering to diverse industrial requirements.</p></p>
