<p><p>The aerospace valves market encompasses several types critical for aircraft operations. Pneumatic valves regulate air pressure and flow in systems like landing gear and brakes. Hydraulic valves control fluid movement in hydraulic systems for flight control surfaces and actuation. Solenoid valves utilize electromagnetic force to manage fluid flow, commonly found in fuel and control systems. The fuel market involves valves that ensure safe and efficient fuel delivery and management, playing a vital role in overall aircraft performance and safety.</p></p>
