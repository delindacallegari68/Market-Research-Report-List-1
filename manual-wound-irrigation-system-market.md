<p><p>A Manual Wound Irrigation System is a medical device used to clean wounds by flushing them with a sterile solution, such as saline or water, to remove debris, bacteria, and dead tissue. This process helps reduce the risk of infection and promotes faster healing. The system typically includes a syringe, nozzle, or bulb to deliver the solution at controlled pressure, ensuring effective wound cleansing without causing further tissue damage.</p><p>The Manual Wound Irrigation System Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic wounds, surgical procedures, and traumatic injuries. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 8.8% during the forecast period.
