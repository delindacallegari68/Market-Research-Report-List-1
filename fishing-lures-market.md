<p><p>The fishing lures market is divided into two main types: freshwater and saltwater fishing lures. Freshwater lures are designed for inland fishing in lakes, rivers, and ponds, targeting species like bass and trout. They typically mimic the appearance and movement of local baitfish and insects. In contrast, saltwater lures are crafted for ocean use, attracting saltwater species like tuna and marlin. These lures are usually more durable and resistant to corrosive saltwater environments, featuring vibrant colors and robust designs.</p></p>
