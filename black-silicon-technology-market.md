The Reactive Ion Etching (RIE) segment utilizes plasma to etch precise patterns on silicon wafers, optimizing solar cell efficiency. In contrast, Metal Catalyzed Chemical Etching (MCCE) employs metal nanoparticles to create nanostructures, further improving light trapping and conversion efficiency. Together, these methods are pivotal in advancing solar energy technologies and enhancing the performance of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
