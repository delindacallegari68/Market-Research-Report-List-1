HPGP sterilization is increasingly favored for its efficacy, speed, and environmentally friendly byproducts, making it suitable for various healthcare applications.</p><p>The market for HPGP sterilizers is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.2% during the forecast period, driven by an increasing emphasis on infection control in healthcare settings and the rising demand for minimally invasive surgeries.
