<p><ul><li>Digital Light Processing (DLP)</li><li>Liquid Crystal Display (LCD)</li><li>Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Floor Type Projector market features three primary technologies: Digital Light Processing (DLP), Liquid Crystal Display (LCD), and Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS). DLP uses tiny mirrors to reflect light, offering sharp images and vibrant colors, ideal for dynamic presentations. LCD, utilizing liquid crystals to project images, provides excellent color accuracy and brightness, suitable for educational and corporate use. LCoS combines the benefits of both DLP and LCD, delivering high resolution and deep contrast, making it favored for high-end applications like home theaters.</p></p>
