<p><p>The Light Automobile Steering Systems market comprises three main types: Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), which utilizes fluid pressure to assist steering, enhancing vehicle maneuverability; Electronic Power Steering (EPS), which employs electric motors and sensors for more precise control and improved energy efficiency; and Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), which combines elements of both HPS and EPS, providing hydraulic assistance while being electronically controlled. Each system offers distinct advantages, catering to different consumer preferences and vehicle designs within the automotive industry.</p></p>
