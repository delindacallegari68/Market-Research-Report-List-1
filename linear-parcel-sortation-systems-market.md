<p><ul><li>Flat Sorters/Bomb Bay Sorters</li><li>Tilt Tray Sorters</li><li>Cross-Belt Sorters (Horizontal)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear parcel sortation systems encompass various types designed for efficient sorting of packages in logistics and warehousing. Flat sorters or bomb bay sorters utilize a flat surface to direct parcels to designated chutes. Tilt tray sorters feature trays that tilt to release items at specific points, enhancing sorting accuracy. Cross-belt sorters utilize horizontal belts that move packages to their destinations, allowing for high-speed sorting and flexibility. Each type addresses specific logistical challenges, optimizing the parcel handling process in different settings.</p></p>
