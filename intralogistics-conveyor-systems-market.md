<p><p>Intralogistics conveyor systems encompass various types essential for efficient material handling. Roller conveyors use rollers to transport goods, ideal for heavy items. Belt conveyors consist of endless belts, suitable for moving a wide range of products. Overhead conveyors utilize a track system to move items above the floor, maximizing space. Pallet conveyors specifically cater to palletized goods, facilitating heavy loads. Other conveyors may include specialized systems like screw or magnetic conveyors, addressing unique operational needs in warehouses and manufacturing.</p></p>
