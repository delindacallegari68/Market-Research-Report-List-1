<p><p>The multilayer ceramic packages market comprises various sealing technologies and types. Ceramic–Metal Sealing (CERTM) ensures robust hermetic seals, ideal for high-performance applications. Glass–Metal Sealing (GTMS) offers effective protection for sensitive components. Passivation glass acts as a protective layer, enhancing the durability of electronic devices. Transponder glass is designed for RFID applications, providing secure data transmission. Reed glass encapsulates magnetic switches, ensuring reliability in harsh environments, making these materials vital for modern electronic packaging solutions.</p></p>
