<p><p>The DC ammeter market can be categorized into analog and digital ammeters. Analog ammeters use a moving coil mechanism to display current readings on a dial, offering simplicity and reliability. They are often preferred for basic applications where precision is less critical. Digital ammeters, on the other hand, utilize electronic components to provide accurate and easily readable measurements on a digital display. They are typically favored in modern applications for their enhanced features, such as data logging and improved accuracy, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
