<p><p>The robotic sanders market is segmented into three main types: orbital sanders, belt sanders, and others. Orbital sanders employ a circular motion to provide a smooth finish, making them ideal for fine sanding on flat surfaces. Belt sanders use a continuous loop of sandpaper for aggressive material removal, suitable for large, flat areas. The "others" category includes specialized sanding equipment designed for unique applications, such as edge sanding or contouring, catering to diverse industrial needs and enhancing efficiency in various processes.</p></p>
