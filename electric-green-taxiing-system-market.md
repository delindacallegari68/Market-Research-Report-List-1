<p><p>The Electric Green Taxiing System (EGTS) market is primarily categorized into two types: On-Board Type and External Systems Type. </p><p>On-Board Type systems integrate electric taxiing capabilities directly into the aircraft, allowing for reduced fuel consumption during ground operations. In contrast, External Systems Type relies on ground-based infrastructure to provide electric power and control for taxiing, often enhancing operational efficiency without altering the aircraft. Both types aim to decrease emissions and improve sustainability in aviation.</p></p>
