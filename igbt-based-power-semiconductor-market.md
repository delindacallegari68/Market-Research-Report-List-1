<p><p>The IGBT-based power semiconductor market is primarily segmented into discrete and module types. Discrete IGBTs are individual components that offer flexibility for design and customization in various applications, such as motor drives and power supplies. In contrast, IGBT modules integrate multiple IGBT chips and auxiliary components into a single package, providing enhanced performance and simplified thermal management. These modules are ideal for high-power applications like renewable energy, industrial drives, and electric vehicles, where efficiency and compactness are crucial.</p></p>
