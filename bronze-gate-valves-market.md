<p><p>The Bronze Gate Valves market is primarily segmented into two connection types: flanged and threaded. Flanged connection valves feature flat extensions on the valve body, allowing for easy bolting to adjoining pipes, ensuring a secure and leak-proof seal. Threaded connection valves, on the other hand, utilize male and female threads for direct attachment to pipes, providing a more compact design ideal for smaller applications. Both types offer reliable performance and durability in various industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
