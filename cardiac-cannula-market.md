<p><strong>The Cardiac Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Venous Cannula</li><li>Arterial Cannula</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac cannula market encompasses two main types: venous cannulas and arterial cannulas. Venous cannulas are designed to facilitate the drainage of deoxygenated blood from the venous system, often used in procedures like cardiopulmonary bypass. In contrast, arterial cannulas are utilized to deliver oxygenated blood into the arterial system, playing a crucial role in maintaining circulation during cardiac surgeries. Both types are integral for effective cardiovascular support and patient management in various surgical interventions.</p></p>
