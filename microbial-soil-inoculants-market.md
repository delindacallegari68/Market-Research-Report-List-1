<p><p>The microbial soil inoculants market is categorized into three main types: bacterial inoculants, fungal inoculants, and composite inoculants. Bacterial inoculants enhance soil fertility and plant growth by promoting beneficial bacteria that improve nutrient availability. Fungal inoculants focus on mycorrhizal fungi, which establish symbiotic relationships with plant roots to boost nutrient uptake. Composite inoculants combine both bacteria and fungi, offering synergistic effects that enhance soil health and crop productivity, making them increasingly popular among farmers seeking sustainable agricultural solutions.</p></p>
