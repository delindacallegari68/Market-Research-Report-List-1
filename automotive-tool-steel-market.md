<p><p>The automotive tool steel market is segmented into three main types: Carbon Tool Steel, Alloy Tool Steel, and High-Speed Tool Steel. Alloy Tool Steel incorporates various alloying elements, enhancing properties like toughness and hardenability, ideal for heavy-duty applications. High-Speed Tool Steel is designed for cutting tools requiring high hardness and resistance to heat, delivering superior performance in high-speed machining operations.</p></p>
