<p><p>The automotive semiconductor test equipment market includes two main types: wafer testing and package testing. Wafer testing involves assessing semiconductor wafers during the manufacturing process to ensure functionality and performance before they are sliced into individual chips. Package testing focuses on evaluating the performance and reliability of the semiconductors once they are encapsulated in protective packaging. Both testing types are essential for ensuring the reliability and quality of automotive electronics, which are critical for safety and performance in vehicles.</p></p>
