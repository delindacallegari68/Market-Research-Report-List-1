<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Flow Tester Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrusive</li><li>Non-Intrusive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic flow tester market is divided into two main types: intrusive and non-intrusive. Intrusive ultrasonic flow testers involve inserting a sensor directly into the flow system to measure fluid velocity and provide accurate readings. In contrast, non-intrusive testers operate externally, using sensors that clamp onto pipes to determine flow without contact. While intrusive methods often deliver higher precision, non-intrusive options offer easier installation and reduced disruption to existing systems, catering to different application needs across various industries.</p></p>
