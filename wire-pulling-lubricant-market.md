<p><p>The wire-pulling lubricant market is categorized into three main types: liquid, wax, and gel. Liquid lubricants are fluid-based and offer easy application for smooth wire pulling, reducing friction and wear. Wax lubricants provide a thicker coating, often used in challenging conditions, enhancing durability. Gel lubricants combine properties of both liquid and wax, offering a semi-viscous option that stays in place while providing effective lubrication. Each type addresses specific needs in wire installation and maintenance, ensuring optimal performance and efficiency.</p></p>
