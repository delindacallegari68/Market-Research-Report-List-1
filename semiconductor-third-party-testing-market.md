<p><strong>The Semiconductor Third-party Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laboratory Testing</li><li>Wafer Testing and Final Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor third-party testing market comprises three main types: laboratory testing, wafer testing, and final testing. Laboratory testing involves verifying the material properties and performance of semiconductor components under controlled conditions. Wafer testing focuses on evaluating individual semiconductor chips on a silicon wafer, ensuring that they function correctly before packaging. Final testing takes place after packaging, assessing the completed semiconductor devices for reliability and performance before they are shipped to customers, ensuring quality and compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
