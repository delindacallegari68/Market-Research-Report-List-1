<p><p>The infrared light-emitting diode (LED) market encompasses two primary types: Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) LEDs and Aluminium Gallium Arsenide (AlGaAs) LEDs. GaAs LEDs are known for their efficiency in converting electrical energy into infrared light, making them ideal for applications such as remote controls and sensors. AlGaAs LEDs, on the other hand, offer a wider range of wavelengths and improved temperature stability, commonly used in optical communication and industrial applications. Both types contribute significantly to advancements in various technology sectors.</p></p>
