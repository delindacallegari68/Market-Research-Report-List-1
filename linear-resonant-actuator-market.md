<p><p>The Linear Resonant Actuator (LRA) market comprises two primary types: rectangular linear resonant actuators and coin-type linear resonant actuators. Rectangular LRAs are typically used in applications requiring larger vibration outputs, ideal for diverse devices including smartphones and wearables. Coin-type LRAs, on the other hand, feature a compact design suitable for smaller applications, providing precise haptic feedback in devices like remote controls and gaming controllers. Both types leverage resonant frequency for efficiency, enhancing user experience through tactile sensations.</p></p>
