<p><strong>The Temporomandibular Joint Implant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mandibular Component</li><li>Fossa Component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Temporomandibular Joint Implant market consists of two primary components: the Mandibular Component and the Fossa Component. The Mandibular Component is designed to replace the lower jawbone, facilitating movement and providing stability during chewing and speaking. In contrast, the Fossa Component replaces the depression in the skull where the jaw articulates, ensuring proper alignment and function of the joint. Together, these components aim to alleviate pain, restore mobility, and improve the overall quality of life for patients with TMJ disorders.</p></p>
