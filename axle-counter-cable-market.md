<p><ul><li>Twisted Pair Cables</li><li>Star-quad Cables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The axle counter cable market features two primary types: twisted pair cables and star-quad cables. Twisted pair cables consist of pairs of insulated wires twisted together, effectively reducing electromagnetic interference and enhancing signal quality, making them suitable for railway applications. Star-quad cables, on the other hand, are designed with four wires arranged in a star-like formation, providing high reliability and improved noise immunity. Both types ensure accurate track monitoring and are essential for efficient railway operations and safety management.</p></p>
