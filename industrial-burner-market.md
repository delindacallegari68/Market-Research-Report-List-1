<p><p>The industrial burner market comprises three main types: oil-based, gas-based, and dual fuel burners. Oil-based burners use liquid fuels, offering high efficiency and suitability for specific applications, such as heavy industry. Gas-based burners utilize natural gas or propane, providing cleaner combustion and lower emissions, making them increasingly popular. Dual fuel burners afford flexibility by operating on either oil or gas, allowing industries to switch fuel sources based on cost and availability, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
