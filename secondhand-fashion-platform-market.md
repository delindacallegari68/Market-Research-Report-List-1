<p><p>Secondhand fashion platforms primarily operate in two market types: B2C (Business to Consumer) and C2C (Consumer to Consumer). In B2C, retailers or brands sell pre-owned clothing directly to consumers, often with quality assurance and brand trust. In contrast, C2C platforms facilitate transactions between individual users, allowing them to buy and sell secondhand items among themselves. Both models promote sustainability, enable savings, and enhance the circular economy by giving new life to pre-loved garments while catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
