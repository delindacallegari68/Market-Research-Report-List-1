<p><strong>The Mobile Payment Data Security Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contactless Tokenisation</li><li>Remote Tokenisation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile payment data security encompasses various techniques used to protect sensitive information during transactions. Contactless tokenization involves generating a unique token to represent payment data for in-person transactions, enhancing security by preventing direct exposure of card details. Remote tokenization, on the other hand, secures data for online transactions by replacing sensitive information with tokens before transmitting it over the internet. Both methods significantly reduce the risk of fraud and data breaches, ensuring safer mobile payment experiences for consumers and businesses.</p></p>
