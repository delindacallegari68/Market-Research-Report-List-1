<p><ul><li>Capacitive Sensor</li><li>Resistance Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Duct humidity sensors measure moisture levels in HVAC systems, with capacitive and resistance sensors being the primary types. Capacitive sensors use a moisture-sensitive dielectric material between electrodes; changes in humidity alter the capacitance, providing accurate readings. Resistance sensors rely on a hygroscopic material that changes electrical resistance with humidity variations. Both types are integral for maintaining optimal indoor air quality and energy efficiency, with capacitive sensors offering higher precision and resistance sensors being cost-effective for basic applications.</p></p>
