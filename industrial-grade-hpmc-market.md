<p><p>The industrial grade HPMC market is categorized into three viscosity types: low, middle, and high viscosity. Low viscosity HPMC is primarily used for applications requiring quick solubility and fluidity, such as in paints and coatings. Middle viscosity HPMC serves moderate thickening and binding functions, often utilized in pharmaceuticals and personal care products. High viscosity HPMC is ideal for demanding applications needing robust thickening and stabilizing properties, commonly found in construction materials and adhesives. Each category fulfills distinct industry requirements.</p></p>
