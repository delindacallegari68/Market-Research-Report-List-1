<p><p>The Air Bath Constant Temperature Shaker market is segmented into vertical and horizontal types based on the orientation of the shaking platform. Vertical shakers operate with a shaking motion that moves up and down, providing effective mixing for liquid samples in flasks or test tubes. In contrast, horizontal shakers utilize a side-to-side movement, which is ideal for more delicate samples or for ensuring even mixing without excessive agitation. Both types ensure precise temperature control for various laboratory applications.</p></p>
