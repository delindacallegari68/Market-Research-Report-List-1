<p><p>Poppet solenoid valves are essential components in fluid control systems, categorized primarily into two market types: normally-closed and normally-open. Normally-closed valves remain shut when de-energized, allowing flow only when activated, making them suitable for safety applications. Conversely, normally-open valves allow continuous flow until energized, ideal for applications requiring fail-safe operation. Both types serve diverse industries, including automation, HVAC, and water management, offering reliability and efficiency in regulating fluid dynamics based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
