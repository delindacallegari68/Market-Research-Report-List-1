<p><strong>The Dark Fibre Networks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Dark Fibre Networks</li><li>Multi-mode Dark Fibre Networks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dark fibre networks refer to unused optical fibre cables that can be leased or sold to organizations for their own telecommunications needs. Single mode dark fibre networks use a single strand of glass fibre to allow for long-distance data transmission with lower signal loss, ideal for high-bandwidth applications. In contrast, multi-mode dark fibre networks utilize multiple strands, enabling shorter distances and providing higher bandwidth at a reduced cost, suitable for data centers and enterprise networks. Both types support scalability and enhanced connectivity.</p></p>
