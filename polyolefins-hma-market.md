<p><p>The polyolefins hot melt adhesives (HMA) market is divided into two main types: ordinary polyolefins HMA and waterproof polyolefins HMA. Ordinary polyolefins HMA are commonly used for general bonding applications, offering good adhesion and thermal stability. In contrast, waterproof polyolefins HMA provide enhanced resistance to moisture and are ideal for applications requiring durability in wet environments, such as packaging and construction. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, textiles, and consumer goods, driven by their versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
