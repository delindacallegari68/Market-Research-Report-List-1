<p><p>The High Temperature Industrial TV System market primarily features two key types of sensors: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). CCD sensors are known for their superior image quality and low noise, making them ideal for high-temperature environments where precision is essential. In contrast, CMOS sensors offer lower power consumption and faster processing speeds, making them suitable for applications requiring real-time imaging. Both sensor types are crucial for enhancing operational safety and efficiency in demanding industrial settings.</p></p>
