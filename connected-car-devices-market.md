<p><p>The Connected Car Devices market includes technologies enabling communication between vehicles and their surroundings. **Vehicle-to-Vehicle (V2V)** allows cars to exchange data, enhancing safety and traffic flow. **Vehicle-to-Infrastructure (V2I)** connects vehicles to road infrastructure like traffic lights, improving navigation and reducing congestion. **Vehicle-to-Pedestrian (V2P)** facilitates communication between vehicles and pedestrians, often via smartphones, to prevent accidents. These systems collectively aim to create safer, smarter, and more efficient transportation ecosystems by leveraging real-time data exchange and connectivity.</p></p>
