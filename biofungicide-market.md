<p><p>The biofungicide market includes various types aimed at controlling plant pathogens. Agricultural antibiotics target bacterial infections in crops, helping to promote healthy plant growth. Bacterium fungicides utilize beneficial bacteria to outcompete harmful pathogens. Fungi fungicides deploy natural fungi that suppress disease-causing fungi, enhancing plant resilience. Virus fungicides are designed to manage viral infections in plants, often using biocontrol agents to limit viral spread. Together, these biofungicides contribute to sustainable agricultural practices and reduce reliance on synthetic chemicals.</p></p>
