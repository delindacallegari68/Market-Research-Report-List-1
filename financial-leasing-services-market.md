<p><ul><li>Capital Lease</li><li>Operating Lease</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The financial leasing services market consists of various types, primarily including capital leases and operating leases. A capital lease allows the lessee to acquire the benefits of ownership and often leads to asset acquisition at the lease's end, affecting balance sheets. In contrast, an operating lease is typically shorter-term, treating leased assets as rentals, thus not reflected on the balance sheet. Other market types include leveraged leases and synthetic leases, offering different financial and operational flexibility for businesses.</p></p>
