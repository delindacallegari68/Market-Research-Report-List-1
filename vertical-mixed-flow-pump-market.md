<p><p>The vertical mixed flow pump market features three main types of impellers: semi-open, open, and enclosed. Semi-open impellers allow some solids to pass through, enhancing versatility in handling various fluids, while open impellers offer a simple design that minimizes clogging. Enclosed impellers provide better efficiency and are ideal for high-pressure applications, as they contain a shroud that improves flow stabilization. Each type serves specific operational needs, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
